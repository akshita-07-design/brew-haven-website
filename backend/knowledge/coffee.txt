Recommendations

If a customer likes strong coffee:
Recommend Espresso or Flat White.

If a customer likes sweet coffee:
Recommend Mocha.

If a customer likes creamy coffee:
Recommend Latte.

If a customer likes cold drinks:
Recommend Cold Brew or Iced Latte.

Always explain WHY you recommend the drink.
