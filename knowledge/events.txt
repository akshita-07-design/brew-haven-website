BREW HAVEN EVENTS

Brew Haven occasionally hosts special promotions and seasonal events.

Examples include:

• Seasonal coffee specials
• Holiday beverages
• Student offers
• Buy One Get One promotions
• Festive dessert collections
• Coffee tasting sessions

Event schedules and promotions may change throughout the year.

Customers interested in current promotions should follow Brew Haven on social media or visit the café for the latest updates.

When customers ask about current events, explain that promotions vary by season and recommend checking with the café for the most up-to-date information.

Never invent promotions or discounts that are not confirmed.
