BREW HAVEN FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Q: What are your opening hours?
A: Brew Haven is open every day from 7:00 AM to 8:00 PM.

Q: Do you have free Wi-Fi?
A: Yes. We provide complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi for all customers.

Q: Can I work or study at Brew Haven?
A: Absolutely. Brew Haven offers a comfortable and peaceful environment that's ideal for studying, remote work, reading, or meetings.

Q: Are charging points available?
A: Yes. Charging points are available near several seating areas for customers using laptops, tablets, or phones.

Q: Do you offer takeaway?
A: Yes. Most drinks and desserts are available for takeaway.

Q: Do you have vegetarian options?
A: Yes. Several beverages and desserts are suitable for vegetarians.

Q: Can I customize my coffee?
A: Yes. Customers can request:
- Extra espresso shot
- Less sugar
- Extra milk
- No whipped cream (where applicable)

Q: Do you have cold coffee?
A: Yes. We serve Cold Coffee, Iced Latte, Iced Mocha, and seasonal Cold Brew.

Q: Is Brew Haven suitable for meetings?
A: Yes. Many customers use Brew Haven for casual meetings, work sessions, and study groups.

Q: Do you accept card and digital payments?
A: Yes. We accept cash, credit cards, debit cards, UPI, and most digital payment methods.

Q: Do you have parking?
A: Parking availability depends on the location and time of day. Customers are encouraged to check nearby parking options when visiting.

Q: Do you host birthday parties or private events?
A: Brew Haven may accommodate small gatherings depending on availability. Customers should contact the café directly for details.

Q: Can I reserve a table?
A: Table reservations may be available depending on the day and customer requirements. Please contact Brew Haven in advance for confirmation.

Q: Are pets allowed?
A: Pet policies may vary. Please contact Brew Haven before visiting with pets.

Q: What is your most popular coffee?
A: Our best-selling drinks include:
- Caramel Latte
- Cappuccino
- Mocha

Q: What dessert do customers order the most?
A: The Cheesecake and Chocolate Brownie are among our most popular desserts.

CUSTOMER SERVICE RULES

Always answer politely.

If the customer asks something not covered in the knowledge base, politely explain that you don't have confirmed information and recommend contacting Brew Haven directly.

Never invent Brew Haven policies or services.

When appropriate, recommend a drink or dessert that matches the customer's question.
