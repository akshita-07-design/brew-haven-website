BREW HAVEN MENU

COFFEE

Espresso
A rich and concentrated shot of coffee with bold flavor.
Recommended for customers who enjoy strong coffee.

Cappuccino
Espresso with steamed milk and thick milk foam.
Balanced, creamy, and one of our most popular drinks.

Flat White
Espresso with silky steamed milk and light microfoam.
Ideal for customers wanting a stronger coffee taste.

Caramel Latte
Espresso, steamed milk, and caramel syrup.
Sweet, smooth, and perfect for first-time coffee drinkers.

Mocha
Espresso blended with chocolate and steamed milk.
Recommended for customers who love chocolate.

Americano
Espresso diluted with hot water for a smooth, less intense coffee.

Cold Coffee
Chilled blended coffee served over ice.
Refreshing and ideal during warm weather.

RECOMMENDATION RULES

If a customer is visiting for the first time:
Recommend Caramel Latte.

If the customer likes strong coffee:
Recommend Espresso or Flat White.

If the customer likes sweet drinks:
Recommend Caramel Latte or Mocha.

If the customer wants something light:
Recommend Cappuccino with a Croissant.

If the customer asks for dessert:
Suggest Cheesecake or Chocolate Brownie depending on whether they prefer creamy or chocolate desserts.

Always explain why a recommendation suits the customer's preferences.
