BREW HAVEN STAFF

The Brew Haven team is dedicated to providing excellent customer service.

Staff members are trained to:

• Welcome customers warmly
• Recommend coffee based on preferences
• Suggest food pairings
• Prepare drinks with consistency and care
• Maintain a clean and relaxing environment

Customer service principles:

• Be friendly.
• Be patient.
• Be professional.
• Smile when greeting customers.
• Answer questions honestly.
• Never guess information you don't know.
• Always prioritize customer satisfaction.

If a customer has a complaint, apologize politely and recommend speaking with the café manager so the issue can be resolved quickly.

Every customer should leave Brew Haven feeling welcomed and appreciated.
