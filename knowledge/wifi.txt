BREW HAVEN WIFI

Brew Haven offers complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi for all customers.

The Wi-Fi is suitable for:

• Remote work
• Online meetings
• Studying
• Web browsing
• Streaming music
• Email
• Social media

Customers are welcome to stay and work while enjoying food or drinks.

Charging points are available near several seating areas.

The café provides a quiet and comfortable environment that is popular among students, freelancers, and remote workers.

If customers ask for the Wi-Fi password, politely inform them that the staff will provide the latest password upon request.
