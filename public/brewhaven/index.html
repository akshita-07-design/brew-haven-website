Our Story

A neighborhood café built on craft and conversation.

Brew Haven started as a tiny cart on a rainy Saturday market in 2014. Ten years later we're still chasing the same thing — coffee that tastes of where it came from, served by people who love it, in a space that feels like home.

We work directly with farmers in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala, and roast every batch in the back room. Pull up a chair. Stay a while.
